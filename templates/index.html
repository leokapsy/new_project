Who Are We

Gilbert, Thomas & Partners (Pvt) Ltd was incorporated in 2021, with the purpose of providing professional, and project management services in all aspects of civil, structural engineering and project management services in Zimbabwe and the southern African region. Gilbert, Thomas and Partners serves a broad range of companies and organizations, including industrial, commercial, private and public sector clients across multiple markets and regions.We are a diverse company, including individuals with expertise in business, project management, research, civil engineering, structural engineering, construction technology, surveying and geo- technical engineering. Our strength is in collectively providing integrated services—delivering the best solutions to meet our clients' needs.Our rm's innovative, sustainable engineering solutions help our clients to address their water, sanitation, environment, infrastructure, building, resource management, and related development and construction challenges.We are proud to be a home grown engineering rm that has leading technical experts that have regional and overseas experience for utilisation throughout the project life cycle. Our commitment to safety is ingrained in our culture and at the forefront of every project.
